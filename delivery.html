Safe & Secure

How we ship your plants

📦

Custom packaging

Every plant is individually wrapped and cushioned in a custom-sized box. Pots are secured with foam inserts. Soil is sealed to prevent spillage. We don't use single-use plastic — just paper, cardboard, and love.

🌡️

Climate aware

We schedule deliveries to avoid peak heat hours. During monsoon season, we add extra waterproofing. Delicate plants like orchids get breathable wrapping to prevent moisture buildup during transit.

📱

Real-time updates

Once your order is dispatched, we'll send you a WhatsApp message with tracking info. You can message us anytime to check on your delivery status. We're always just a message away.

🔄

Damage guarantee

If your plant or pot arrives damaged during transit, send us photos within 24 hours. We'll replace it at no extra cost — no questions asked. We stand behind every delivery.
